Project Description

Univentures is a centralized web platform where University of Michigan students across disciplines can easily connect for the purpose of team building and idea generation.

This project was based on Alex's personal painpoint of the difficulty of connecting with technical talents outside of his immediate network. While team building is one of the first, if not the first barrier to entry in launching a startup, there is no platform that allows for students to connect with each other at the University of Michigan. To solve this pain point, we spent nearly 3 months and hundreds of hours developing this platform and launched the initial version in October 2017.

Throughout this process, we also talked with nearly 20 people involved in various entrepreneurial programs across campus including Tech Arb, Innovation in Action, Innovate Blue, Zell Lurie Institue, Center for Entrepreneurship, Desai Accelerator as well as faculty members to validate the idea and discuss the feasibility of the project.

While we gained positive feedback, user traction stopped growing few weeks after the launch. We realized that we had not conducted enough customer discovery and thus, did not have a thorough understanding of the users' problems and needs. To build the 'right' product, we shut down the platform (it's currently filled with seed data) and are in the process of conducting usability testing.
